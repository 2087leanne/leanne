Artist Introduction

黃麗穎 HUANG LI YING

Since 2020, she has been studying for a Bachelor of Arts in Art and Design at National Tsing Hua University. In 2021, she transferred to the Bachelor's program in the School of Arts, specializing in Technology Art. Having attended an art class for ten years, she has a solid foundation in art techniques. Recently, she has been focusing on researching new media art, exploring the possibilities of art kinetic installations, mechanical and electronic hardware control, and developing artworks in bio-art and augmented mixed reality, conveying ideas through new media of technology art.

She is a new media artist, primarily presenting her works in kinetic sculptures and graphic design. Her works often explore human social issues, and she excels in designing structurally aesthetic art to depict explorations of life problems.

 

於2020年起於國立清華大學就讀藝術與設計學士，2021轉系至藝術學院學士班科技藝術組。曾就讀十年的美術班，以穩固術科基礎，近期專攻研究新媒體藝術，嘗試探究藝術動力裝置機械、以及電子硬體控制的可能性，也在生物藝術與擴增混合實境上發展藝術作品，藉由科技藝術來傳達理念的新媒體

是一位新媒體藝術家，主要的作品呈現方式為動力雕塑以及平面設計，她的作品多在探討人類社會問題，擅長設計結構美感藝術，以描繪人生問題探索的作品呈現。
