Chaos


The lingering sound of the bell brings tranquility amidst the chaos. Here, we contemplate the complex relationship between the positives and negatives of faith. Despite being hurt by the superstitions of those around us, we still seek faith in times of confusion for solace.

The inner mixed emotions are embodied in a concrete way in the work.

 

Servo Motor / Bowl / Photopolymerization Printing

伺服馬達 / 缽/  光固化列印
-
 《掙扎》
-
鐘聲餘音繞樑，在混沌中帶來了寧靜。於此我們思考著信仰的正面與負面之間的複雜關係，儘管因身旁人們的迷信而曾感到受傷，我們仍在迷失時尋求信仰以得到心靈撫慰。

內在的混合情感以具象的方式在作品中體現。
